Rules

The players can choose whether to flip the coin or not,

but they DON'T GET TO SEE THE OUTCOME.

Each player has two chances to flip the coin or not.

Wait, did you say "Who plays first?"

Oh, That's a great question. The computer plays first!

After these four rounds, the system will reveal the coin.

And if it is Heads, then you win.

But if the coin is Tails, then you lose.

Simple, right?

New! A Wizard!

Now, there's a wizard to tell you the state of the coin
after the computer makes its choice!
But he is not a Mad-Eye Moody.
He is more like a Ron with his broken wand.
At first, the wizard can tell you the right state of the coin with 60% of confidence.
But, the wizard can tell you the right sate of the coin
with 55% of confidence in his second try.
